Abstract

An apparatus (10) for covering a vehicle emplacement, in particular a bicycle, comprises: a pair of extendible uprights (20), each of which is connectable to a portion of a vehicle and is movable between a rest configuration and an operating configuration; at least one abutment element (30) solidly associated with a portion of an upright (20); a covering (40) that is configurable between a compact condition and a stretched condition. Furthermore, the covering (40) is slidably movable along a movement direction (M) that extends between an end (21) and the abutment element (30) of each upright (20) to come into contact with each abutment element (30) for pulling it in the movement direction (M) and extending each upright (20) to cover the vehicle emplacement.
The subject matter of the present patent application is also a method for the implementation of the aforesaid apparatus.
