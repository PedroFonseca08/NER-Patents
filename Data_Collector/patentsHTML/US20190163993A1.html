Abstract

Disclosed is a lane maintaining method and apparatus, the method includes determining a presence of a tunnel in front of a vehicle, detecting a first lighting pattern in the tunnel from a first front view image acquired from the vehicle before the vehicle enters the tunnel in response the tunnel being present, determining reference information on a current driving lane of the vehicle based on the first lighting pattern, detecting a second lighting pattern in the tunnel from a second front view image acquired after the vehicle enters the tunnel, and determining whether the vehicle departs from the current driving lane based on the second lighting pattern and the reference information.
